The Problem with "Free" Fitness Apps

Most "free" fitness apps are free in name only. The workout plan is free — but the workouts require a subscription. The meal tracker is free — but the recipes are locked. The real features cost $10–$20/month.

NoxFit is different. The core features — workouts, meal plans, nutrition tracking, grocery budgeting — are genuinely free, with no time limits or hidden paywalls.

What Beginners Actually Need

  • A clear, simple workout plan — not 50 options with no guidance
  • Exercise demonstrations — video or animated, not just a name
  • Basic nutrition guidance — not advanced macro cycling
  • Progress tracking — to see improvement over time
  • Consistency tools — reminders, streaks, habit tracking

Frequently Asked Questions

Will NoxFit ever charge me? +
NoxFit's core features are free and always will be. There are no hidden charges, no trial periods that expire, and no credit card required to download.
Is NoxFit good for older adults or people with injuries? +
Yes. NoxFit offers low-impact workout options and allows you to flag injuries or limitations during setup. Always consult your doctor before starting a new exercise program if you have a medical condition.
How is NoxFit different from MyFitnessPal or Lose It? +
Those apps focus on calorie tracking. NoxFit combines workout planning, meal planning, nutrition tracking, AND grocery budgeting in one app — making it a complete fitness and health solution rather than just a food diary.
Does NoxFit work offline? +
Yes — NoxFit is designed to work offline. You can access your workout plan and log sessions without an internet connection, which is perfect for gyms with poor signal.
