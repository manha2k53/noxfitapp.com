Why We Built NoxFit

Most fitness apps are built for people who already know what they're doing. Complex programs, intimidating interfaces, and $20/month subscriptions — designed for the 10%, not the 90%.

We started NoxFit with a simple belief: getting healthy should be accessible to everyone, regardless of your fitness level, background, or budget.

So we built an app that meets you where you are. Whether you're starting your very first workout or training for your fourth marathon — NoxFit gives you clear, personalized guidance with no judgment and no confusion.

And because we know that healthy eating can be expensive, we went further — adding meal planning and grocery budgeting tools so you can reach your health goals without breaking the bank.

Our Journey

From Idea to
500,000 Users

NoxFit didn't start as a big company. It started as a problem we needed to solve for ourselves.

💡

2021 — The Problem

Our founder Alex tried 7 different fitness apps and quit every one of them. They were too complicated, too expensive, or too generic. He decided to build the one he actually wanted to use.

🔨

2022 — First Version

NoxFit launched with just workouts and basic nutrition tracking. The focus was simplicity — clean design, zero friction, and a plan that actually adapts to your life.

🚀

2023 — Meal Planning Added

Users told us that food was the hardest part. So we built a full meal planning system — 5,000+ recipes, automatic weekly plans, and diet-type filters.

🛒

2024 — Grocery Budgeting

"Healthy food is too expensive" was the #1 reason users said they couldn't commit. We fixed that with smart grocery lists and budget tracking built directly from your meal plan.

🌍

Today — 500K+ Users, 42 Countries

Half a million people now use NoxFit every month across 42 countries. We're still free at the core and always will be — because that's the whole point.
