It started as a problem we needed to solve for ourselves. 💡 2021 — The Problem Our founder Alex tried 7 different fitness apps and quit every one of them. They were too complicated, too expensive, or too generic. He decided to build the one he actually wanted to use. 🔨 2022 — First Version NoxFit launched with just workouts and basic nutrition tracking. The focus was simplicity — clean design, zero friction, and a plan that actually adapts to your life. 🚀 2023 — Meal Planning Added Users told us that food was the hardest part. So we built a full meal planning system — 5,000+ recipes, automatic weekly plans, and diet-type filters. 🛒 2024 — Grocery Budgeting "Healthy food is too expensive" was the #1 reason users said they couldn't commit. We fixed that with smart grocery lists and budget tracking built directly from your meal plan. 🌍 Today — 500K+ Users, 42 Countries Half a million people now use NoxFit every month across 42 countries.
