Why Most People Overspend on Groceries

  • No list — shopping without a list leads to 40% more impulse spending
  • No meal plan — buying ingredients without a plan means half end up in the bin
  • No tracking — if you don't know what you spent, you can't improve it
  • Convenience trap — pre-made and packaged foods cost 3–5x more per serving than home-cooked

5 Habits That Cut Your Grocery Bill Fast

  • Shop once a week — multiple trips per week = multiple opportunities to impulse buy
  • Eat before you shop — hunger leads to $20–$30 of extra spending every time
  • Use the whole ingredient — if a recipe uses half a can, plan another meal using the other half
  • Buy in-season produce — seasonal vegetables cost 50–70% less and taste better
  • Check your fridge first — always do a quick audit before writing your shopping list
